Chlorine pool chemistry notes

FC minimum with CYA

Keep FC ≥ CYA÷7.5 to prevent algae. At CYA 40 ppm, minimum FC = 5.3 ppm. Shock level (SLAM) = CYA × 0.2.

CYA caution

CYA builds up from trichlor tablets — test monthly. Above 80 ppm it severely reduces chlorine effectiveness. Drain and dilute; no chemical fix.

Shock (SLAM) threshold

Shock level = CYA × 0.2 (lower than SWG pools). At CYA 40, shock FC = 8 ppm. Hold until CC < 0.5 ppm and pool is clear.

Granular shock (Cal-hypo)

Cal-hypo raises calcium hardness over time. Track CH if shocking frequently. Pre-dissolve in a bucket before adding to pool.

Muriatic acid safety

Always add acid to water, never water to acid. Pour slowly near a return jet with pump running. Wait 30 min to retest.

TDS management

TDS rises as chemicals are added. Above 1500 ppm efficacy drops. Above 2500 ppm a partial drain/refill is recommended.
